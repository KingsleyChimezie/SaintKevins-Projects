Welcome to the official website of the known YouTube Channel:

Virtual Pro Kings (VPK)

My name is Kingsley Chimezie and I am the creator and owner of Virtual Pro Kings...

Virtual Pro Kings started off on YouTube.com, May 3rd 2013. It is a channel dedicated to providing various videos and other content based on EA Sports: FIFA Virtual Pro game mode. Initially I wanted to name my channel "Virtual Pro Community" or "Virtual Pro Club" but those names weren't available, so I came up with the last part "Kings" to replace community or Club. When I thought of Kings I saw VPK as a community with a lot of Elite Virtual Pro gamer's. I am a Virtual Pro fanatic and I love playing the game mode since the first time EA introduced it to FIFA.

Unfortunately unlike FIFA Ultimate Team, there are very few videos or other content dedicated to Virtual Pro game mode. However Virtual Pro has a lot of potential and I can see it growing and becoming more popular due to it's uniqueness. My aim is to provide as many Virtual Pro content as I can for those who love Virtual Pro game mode just like I do. VPK is not just about my work, it's also about the Virtual Pro Community, so content produced by others can also be involved with the name VPK. If you have any decent content(s) you would like to share just contact me. Thank you for visiting my website!
